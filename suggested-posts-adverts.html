Suggested Posts & Adverts

Suggested posts and adverts have slowly been taking over social media applications with them becoming a prominent feature. On some applications, suggested content is kept to a separate section of the application but on others it is front and centre. These posts are often hard to tell apart from regular posts and can sometimes show the user content that can have a negative effect on their emotions and create feelings such as FOMO.

Post Banners

Both Instagram and Facebook should implement banners and warnings that clearly show when a post is suggested or an advert, this would help to create a user who is more aware and by placing the warnings at the top of a post it would allow the user to skip past it without taking notice of the content if they do not wish to interact with suggested content or adverts.
